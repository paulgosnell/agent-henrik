Instagram Feed Setup

Configure Instagram integration for homepage display

View Site

Instagram Access Token

Enter your Instagram Basic Display API access token to display your latest posts on the homepage.

Setup Instructions

  1. Go to Facebook Developers and create a new app
  2. Add "Instagram Basic Display" product to your app
  3. Under "User Token Generator", click "Generate Token" for your Instagram account
  4. Authorize the app to access your Instagram account
  5. Copy the generated access token and paste it above
  6. Click "Save Token" to enable Instagram feed on your homepage

Note: Access tokens expire after 60 days. You'll need to regenerate and update the token periodically.
