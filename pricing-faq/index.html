How We Price Our Services

Planning & Curation Fee

Our base planning fee starts at €2,500 per journey. This covers in-depth consultation, bespoke itinerary design, coordination with local storytellers, and 24/7 concierge support throughout your trip.

Experience Investment

Total journey costs typically range from €5,000 to €25,000+ per person, depending on duration, accommodation level, exclusive access, and seasonal factors. Private guides, luxury transportation, and unique experiences are included.

Corporate & Incentive Pricing

Group experiences and corporate programs are priced per participant. Contact us for detailed proposals tailored to group size, program objectives, and desired impact. Minimum group size: 6 participants.

Note: All pricing is transparent and confirmed before booking. No hidden fees. Payment plans available for journeys exceeding €10,000.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 3-6 months in advance for optimal availability, especially during peak seasons (June-August, December-January for Northern Lights). Last-minute journeys can sometimes be arranged depending on availability.

What's included in the journey cost?

Most journeys include luxury accommodation, private transportation, experiences and activities, private guides, select meals, and 24/7 concierge support. International flights and personal expenses are typically not included unless specifically arranged.

Can I customize my itinerary?

Absolutely. Every journey is fully customized to your interests, pace, and preferences. We start with a detailed consultation to understand your travel style, then craft a bespoke itinerary just for you.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 90+ days before departure receive a full refund minus the planning fee. 60-89 days: 50% refund. Within 60 days: non-refundable. We strongly recommend comprehensive travel insurance, which we can help arrange.

Do you arrange group trips?

Yes, we specialize in private group experiences for families, friends, and corporate teams. Group sizes range from intimate gatherings (4-8) to larger incentive programs (up to 50 participants).

What makes Luxury Travel Sweden different?

We're locals who live and breathe Swedish culture. Our network of storytellers—chefs, artists, designers, wilderness guides—provides authentic insider access you won't find elsewhere. Plus, our AI concierge LIV is available 24/7 to enhance your journey.

What if I have dietary restrictions or accessibility needs?

We accommodate all dietary requirements and accessibility needs. Please share these details during your initial consultation so we can design your journey accordingly and brief all partners in advance.

Can I extend my trip to other Nordic countries?

Yes! We can design multi-country Nordic journeys incorporating Norway, Denmark, Finland, and Iceland. Our network extends throughout Scandinavia, ensuring the same level of curated excellence everywhere you go.
